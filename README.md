## Datasets

| Dataset | Source | Year | Licence | Description |
|----------|----------------|------|----------|----------------------|
| **General Election Results** | [House of Commons Library](https://www.electoralcommission.org.uk/) | 2010, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2024 | [Open Parliment Licence](https://www.parliament.uk/site-information/copyright-parliament/open-parliament-licence/) | UK general election results by parlimentary electoral constituency. |
| **Local Election Results** | [House of Commons Library](https://www.electoralcommission.org.uk/) | 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024 | [Open Parliment Licence](https://www.parliament.uk/site-information/copyright-parliament/open-parliament-licence/) | UK local election results by electoral ward. |
| **Population Estimates** | [Office for National Statistics](https://www.ons.gov.uk/peoplepopulationandcommunity/populationandmigration/populationestimates) | 2020 | [Open Government Licence v3.0](http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/) | Ward-level population estimates including age and male-female gender. |
| **Ethnicity** | [Office for National Statistics](https://www.ons.gov.uk/datasets/TS021/editions/2021/versions/3) | 2021 | [Open Government Licence v3.0](http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/) | Ethnic group population classification for England and Wales by local authority district. |
| **House Price** | [Office for National Statistics](https://www.ons.gov.uk/peoplepopulationandcommunity/housing/datasets/medianpricepaidbywardhpssadataset37) | 1995-2023 | [Open Government Licence v3.0](http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/) | Median house price paid by ward for England and Wales for trend analysis. |
| **Income** | [Office for National Statistics](https://www.ons.gov.uk/employmentandlabourmarket/peopleinwork/earningsandworkinghours/datasets/placeofworkbylocalauthorityashetable7) | 2025 | [Open Government Licence v3.0](http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/) | Annual estimates of paid hours worked and earnings for employees in England and Wales. |
| **Crime** | [Office for National Statistics](https://www.ons.gov.uk/peoplepopulationandcommunity/crimeandjustice/datasets/policeforceareadatatables) | 2025 | [Open Government Licence v3.0](http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/) | Police recorded crime figures by local authority district. |
| **Westminister Parlimentary Wards (Boundaries)** | [ONS Open Geography Portal](https://geoportal.statistics.gov.uk/) | December 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024 | [Open Government Licence v3.0](http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/) | UK electoral ward boundaries. |
| **Local Authority Districts (Boundaries)** | [ONS Open Geography Portal](https://geoportal.statistics.gov.uk/) | December 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025 | [Open Government Licence v3.0](http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/) | UK local authority district boundaries. |
| **Westminister Parlimentary Constituencies (Boundaries)** | [ONS Open Geography Portal](https://geoportal.statistics.gov.uk/) | July 2015, 2017, 2019, 2024 | [Open Government Licence v3.0](http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/) | UK parliamentary electoral constituency boundaries. |
