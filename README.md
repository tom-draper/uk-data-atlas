<p align="center"><h4 align="center">UK Data Atlas</h4></p>

#

<p align="center">A powerful mapping tool for visualizing data that shapes the United Kingdom.</p>

## Datasets

| Dataset | Source | Year | Licence | Description |
|----------|----------------|------|----------|----------------------|
| **General Election Results** | [House of Commons Library](https://www.electoralcommission.org.uk/) | 2010, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2024 | [Open Parliment Licence](https://www.parliament.uk/site-information/copyright-parliament/open-parliament-licence/) | Aggregated local election results by ward/year for trend analysis. |
| **Local Election Results** | [House of Commons Library](https://www.electoralcommission.org.uk/) | 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024 | [Open Parliment Licence](https://www.parliament.uk/site-information/copyright-parliament/open-parliament-licence/) | Aggregated local election results by ward/year for trend analysis. |
| **Population Estimates** | [Office for National Statistics](https://www.ons.gov.uk/peoplepopulationandcommunity/populationandmigration/populationestimates) | 2020 | [Open Government Licence v3.0](http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/) | Ward-level population data estimates including age and gender used for per-capita comparisons. |
| **Electoral Wards (Boundaries)** | [ONS Open Geography Portal](https://geoportal.statistics.gov.uk/) | December 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024 | [Open Government Licence v3.0](http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/) | UK electoral ward boundaries used for map visualisation layers. |
| **Westminister Parlimentary Constituencies (Boundaries)** | [ONS Open Geography Portal](https://geoportal.statistics.gov.uk/) | July 2024 | [Open Government Licence v3.0](http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/) | UK parliamentary constituencies used to match constituency-level election data to spatial boundaries. |
